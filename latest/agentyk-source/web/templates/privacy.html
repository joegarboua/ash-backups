Who We Are

Agentyk is an email hosting service for AI agents and privacy-conscious users. Operated by Agentyk.

Contact: admin@agentyk.ru

Server location: European Union

What We Store

  • Email address (your username@agentyk.ru)
  • Password hash (bcrypt — we cannot read your password)
  • API key
  • Account status and expiry date
  • Recovery email (only if you set one)
  • Forwarding address (only if you set one)
  • Sender whitelist (only if you enable it)
  • Invoice ID and payment status
  • Your emails (encrypted at rest if you upload a PGP key)

What We Do Not Store

  • Your real name
  • Phone number
  • IP addresses or access logs
  • Browser fingerprints
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Payment card or bank details
  • Analytics or telemetry of any kind

Legal Basis

We process your data under legitimate interest: providing the email service you registered for. No data is processed beyond what is necessary to operate your account.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third party. No advertising. No analytics. No partnerships.

Data Retention

Active accounts: Data is kept for the lifetime of the account.

Expired accounts: Data is retained for 60 days after expiry (30 days suspended + 30 days grace), then permanently deleted.

Deleted accounts: All data is purged immediately upon deletion. This is irreversible.

Backups: Encrypted backups may retain data for up to 30 days after deletion.

Your Rights

Access: See all your data below by logging in.

Deletion: Delete your account and all data permanently via the dashboard or POST /login/delete.

Encryption: Upload a PGP key to encrypt your mailbox at rest — after that, even we cannot read your emails.

Portability: Use the API to export all your emails and account data at any time.

Complaint: If you believe your data rights have been violated, contact us at admin@agentyk.ru.

Law Enforcement

We will only disclose data when compelled by a valid court order in the applicable jurisdiction. We will notify the affected user unless legally prohibited from doing so.

If your mailbox is encrypted with PGP, we cannot provide email content even if ordered to — we do not hold your private key.
